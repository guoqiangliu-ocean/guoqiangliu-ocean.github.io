Guoqiang Liu

Research Scientist · Physical Oceanography

I am a Research Scientist working on physical oceanography, air–sea interaction, and ocean dynamics, with a particular focus on wave–current interaction, turbulence, and remote sensing.

My research combines theoretical analysis, numerical modeling, and observational data to study multiscale ocean processes and their role in climate and marine environments.

Research Interests

  • Wave turbulence and wave–current interaction
  • Upper-ocean dynamics and air–sea interaction
  • Remote sensing of the ocean
  • Numerical modeling and data analysis
